Egg Roll Noodle Bowl

Prep/Total time: 30 min.

Yield: 4 servings

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground turmeric
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 6 cups shredded cabbage
  • 2 cups shredded carrots
  • 4 ounces of Thai Kitchen rice noodles
  • 3 green onions, thinly sliced

Directions:

  1. In a cast iron skillet or other large heavy skillet, heat oil over medium high heat; cook and crumble pork until browned, 4-6 minutes.
  2. Stir in soy sauce, garlic and seasonings. Add cabbage and carrots; cook until vegetables are tender, stirring occasionally, 4-6 minutes longer.
  3. Cook rice noodles according to package directions; drain and immediately add to pork mixture, tossing to combine. Sprinkle with green onions.

*If desired, serve with additional soy sauce.
